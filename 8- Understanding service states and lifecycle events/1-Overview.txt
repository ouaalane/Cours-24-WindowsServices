Overview
In this section; we will explore the Windows Service Lifecycle and the key states a service transitions through Starting, Running, Paused, Stopping, and more. We'll also cover the corresponding lifecycle events like OnStart, OnStop, OnPause, OnContinue, and OnShutdown to understand their roles in managing these transitions.

Understanding the interaction between service states and lifecycle events is crucial for effective service management, troubleshooting, and development.
