The SCM can be configured to automatically attempt recovery actions when a service fails.

Recovery Options:
Restart the Service: SCM restarts the service after a failure.
Run a Program: Execute a custom program or script when the service fails.
Reboot the Computer: Restart the system if the service fails repeatedly.
How SCM Handles Failures:
Service Recovery Options:
Automatically restart the service after failure.
Run a custom program or send an alert.
Configurable in services.msc under the Recovery tab.
Failure Detection:
SCM monitors services and transitions them to the "Stopped" state if they crash or fail.
