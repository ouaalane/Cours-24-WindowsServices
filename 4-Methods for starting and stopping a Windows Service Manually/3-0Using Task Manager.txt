2. Using Task Manager
Steps:
Open Task Manager: Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc or Ctrl + Alt + Del and select Task Manager.
Go to the Services tab.
Locate the service: Scroll through the list or click Open Services at the bottom to launch services.msc.
Start or stop the service: Right-click the service and choose Start or Stop.
Advantages:
Convenient for quick access.
Allows switching to services.msc for detailed management.
