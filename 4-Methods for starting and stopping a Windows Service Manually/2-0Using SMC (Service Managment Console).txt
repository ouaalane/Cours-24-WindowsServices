1. Using Services Management Console (services.msc)
Steps:
Open the Services Management Console: Press Win + R, type services.msc, and press Enter.
Locate the service: Scroll through the list to find the desired service by its Display Name.
Start the service: Right-click the service and select Start.
Stop the service: Right-click the service and select Stop.
Restart the service (optional): Right-click the service and select Restart.
Advantages:
User-friendly graphical interface.
Provides additional information about the service (e.g., dependencies, startup type).
